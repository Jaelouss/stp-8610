How to play

Different ways to play

Simply swap and match three or more candies of the same type to clear levels. Use boosters to help with tricky challenges, and aim for high scores. Complete each level by meeting objectives and enjoy daily rewards to boost your progress. Play at your own pace!

    • Swap & Match

      Drag to swap candies and match three or more of the same kind.

    • Create Special Combos

      Combine power-ups for massive explosions!

    • Complete Level Goals

      Finish each stage by achieving specific objectives.

    • Use Boosters Wisely

      Strategically deploy boosters to tackle tricky puzzles.

    • Unlock New Challenges

      Progress through levels to discover exciting new mechanics!
